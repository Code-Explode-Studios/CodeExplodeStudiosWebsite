Whats New Section

Two new games are in development!

Two major titles are currently in development behind the scenes: Charlie’s Rage Stage and The Back Rooms: No Escape. Charlie’s Rage Stage is a wild, immersive VR experience set inside a volatile televised arena, where players are thrown into the spotlight and must navigate explosive social conflicts and live audience reactions in real-time. It’s unpredictable, theatrical, and unlike anything you've ever played. Meanwhile, The Back Rooms: No Escape drags players deep into a warped, endless maze of eerie corridors and uncanny environments, testing nerves and perception in a relentless psychological horror descent. Both games promise entirely unique journeys, one full of manic spectacle, the other soaked in atmospheric dread and they’re coming soon to turn your gaming reality completely on its head.

RC Renegades : High Voltage

Due to unfortunate circumstances RC Renegades : High Voltage has been placed on hold and Due to difficulties with the Google Play Store. It's very likely that RC Renegades : High Voltage will now only be coming to the windows platform.

Ash Vegas : Windows

Due to unforeseen challenges and the ambitious scope of the project, development on Ash Vegas has been temporarily placed on hold. With current resources focused on completing several smaller titles, we’ve made the difficult decision to pause production for the time being. However, we remain fully committed to revisiting Ash Vegas in the future, once our development schedule clears and the team is better equipped to bring the project to life as originally envisioned.

Virus Gunner : Android

Virus Gunner has been removed from the Google Play Store following recent changes to Google’s developer policy guidelines. As a result, the game is no longer accessible through the Play Store platform. An archived version remains available on our website; however, please note that certain features may not function as intended due to the deactivation of Google AdMob services, which previously powered in-game advertisements. We appreciate your understanding and continued support as we evaluate future distribution options.
