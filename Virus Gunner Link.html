Virus Gunner : Released 12/06/2020 - Removed From Google Play Store 2024

Platfoms : Android

Introducing Virus Gunner, the thrilling shooter game where you play as a man on a mission to protect the world from deadly virus strains! Your job is to move around the lab and shoot medicine at incoming virus balls, stopping them from infiltrating your lab. With 50 exciting ranks to conquer, you'll unlock new power-ups that will aid you in your quest to save the world. Keep an eye out for crates that fall from the sky - when they hit the ground and break, they reveal a power-up that you can collect, such as ambulance ammo, a shield, a nuke, or a turret that fires autonomously at the enemy viruses.
