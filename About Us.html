About us

Welcome to Code Explode Studios, where creativity meets innovation in the world of indie media creation. We're a tight-knit team passionate about 3D modeling and game development, dedicated to crafting immersive experiences that captivate audiences. At the core of Code Explode Studios is our visionary CEO, Jay Pacman. With over a decade of hands-on experience in game development and 3D modeling, Jay drives our team forward with expertise and determination. While we may be a small studio, our ambition knows no bounds. With three dynamic game titles in our portfolio and one successful publication, we're just getting started. Our pipeline is brimming with exciting projects poised to make their mark in the industry, and we're thrilled to share them with you. Join us on our journey as we continue to push the boundaries of creativity and engineer unforgettable gaming experiences to the world. We are at the start of our life cycle, so the best, most innovative software is yet to come, but we already have some groundbreaking titles in the works!
