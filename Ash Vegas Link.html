Ash Vegas: In Development

Welcome to Ash Vegas, where the streets pulse with energy and danger lurks around every corner. In this adrenaline-fueled tale, you'll step into the shoes of Jay Pacman, fresh from a brutal breakup and ready to take on the world with grit and determination. It's 2014, and life's a rollercoaster of adrenaline and chaos. You'll face tough choices as you claw your way out of the wreckage of your past. Will you fight tooth and nail against those who seek to bring you down, or will you carve your own path through the madness? Life in Ash Vegas is a non-stop thrill ride. From dodging bullets in dark alleys to striking deals in smoke-filled backrooms, danger is your constant companion. But with every close call and daring escapade, you'll grow bolder and more resilient. But survival isn't enough. To truly thrive, you'll need to embrace the chaos and dive headfirst into the gritty underbelly of the town. Making alliances with dangerous individuals and engaging in activities that push the boundaries of legality. The journey to the top is fraught with peril, but the rewards are worth the risk. In Ash Vegas, every decision you make shapes your destiny. Will you let the chaos consume you, or will you rise from the ashes to become the undisputed kingpin of the streets? The town awaits. What legacy will you leave behind?
