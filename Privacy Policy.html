Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Code Explode Studios, we take the privacy and security of our customers' data seriously. We do not collect any personal data about our users or their devices without their explicit consent. We are committed to being open and transparent about how we collect, use, and store any data that we do collect.

What Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data about our users or their devices. We do not use cookies or any other tracking technology to collect data about our users.

How We Use Your Information

As we do not collect any personal data about our users, we do not use it for any purpose.

Sharing Your Information

As we do not collect any personal data about our users, we do not share it with any third-party entities.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

If we ever need to collect personal data about our users, we will update our privacy policy to reflect these changes. We will also inform our users about these changes and obtain their explicit consent before collecting any personal data.

Contact us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or how we handle your data, please feel free to contact us at our company email, which can be found on the Contact us page.
